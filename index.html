Background

Translating complexity
into clarity

I've spent my career at the intersection of deep technical knowledge and clear communication. Based in Barnstaple, UK, I'm Chief Technologist at Civica / Content Master, where I've created technical content for Microsoft and enterprise audiences since 2007.

My work spans technical documentation, instructional design, content strategy and training delivery — across formats from Microsoft Learn modules and instructor-led courses to white papers, case studies and video scripts.

What sets me apart is genuine technical fluency. I don't just write about Azure, SQL Server or AI platforms — I work in them, validate content hands-on, and attend product JTAs with Microsoft engineering teams. That depth means content that's not just clear, but accurate.

I've also led and mentored teams of up to 16 technical authors across large-scale programmes, bringing the same discipline I apply to my own writing to managing quality, consistency and deadlines at scale.
